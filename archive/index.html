About

I'm a designer and front-end developer based in Portland, Oregon. For the past eight years, I've worked with startups and established brands to create websites, apps, and design systems that are both beautiful and functional.

My approach is simple: listen carefully, question assumptions, and build solutions that solve real problems. I believe the best digital products are the ones you barely notice because they just work.
